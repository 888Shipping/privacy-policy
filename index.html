Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

This privacy policy describes how 888shipping LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Android application, 888-Shipping (the "App").

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Includes name, email, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Usage Data: How you use the App, including features accessed and usage statistics.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS, unique identifiers, and network info.
  • Location Information: With consent, precise location data to facilitate shipping and tracking.

How We Use Your Information

  • Monitor usage trends

  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

Data Retention

We retain personal information as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Rights

Depending on location, you may access, correct, delete, or restrict your data. Contact us at the email below to exercise your rights.

Security

Children's Privacy

The App is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data. Contact us if a child has shared personal information.
